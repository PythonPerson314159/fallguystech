How to perform the “chicken buffer”

  1. Get the rubber chicken powerup.
  2. Dive off the ledge.
  3. Use the chicken powerup.
  4. Dive - again! That’s right, when you use the chicken powerup, the game resets your dive count.
  5. Repeat step 3 and 4 for as long as you can or until your reach your target.

Performance tips

  • If you want to cover a significant amount of extra distance with this trick, Step 3 and 4 must be done in quick succesion. If the only major time you’re not doing anything is when you’re waiting for the power-up cooldown to finish, you’re doing this correctly.
  • Using PlayStation remotes, I find that sliding your finger across from circle to square works very well for performing those critical two steps.

Happy buffering!
